## Description

The engine computes, simulates, iterates, solves and optimizes over a typed-property graph while knowing nothing about system design — and is validated by the test harness. Covers core types, the solve/fixpoint loop, DataScript/MiniZinc/clingo adapters, simulation, and the property/differential/golden test harness.
